About

I grew up reading SF, and keep up to date with current authors. My current favorites are Charles Stross, Greg Egan, Neal Stephenson, Stephen Baxter, and Peter Watts. Passed favs are Iain M Banks, Octavia Butler, & Robert Heinlein.

I started the Beijing Science Fiction Reading Group and ran the Beijing Go Club for 6 years.
